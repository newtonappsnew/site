Privacy Policy

Our privacy policy is simple because our approach to data is simple: we don't collect it. Below is the complete policy for all Newton Apps products, written in plain language.

01
Data We Collect

Newton Apps collects no personal data whatsoever. We do not collect names, emails, device identifiers, usage analytics, crash reports sent to our servers, or any information that could identify you as an individual.

02
Your Documents & Files

All documents, scans, and files you process within our apps are stored exclusively on your device. They are never transmitted to external servers, third-party services, or Newton Apps infrastructure. We have no access to your files — by design.

03
Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate advertising SDKs, analytics platforms, or tracking frameworks. The only third-party interaction is the Apple App Store for purchase verification, which is governed entirely by Apple's own privacy policy.

04
Apple Frameworks

We use Apple's native frameworks (Vision, Core Data, SwiftData, StoreKit) exclusively. These operate locally on your device. Apple's standard iOS privacy protections — camera access, photo library access — require your explicit permission.

05
Children's Privacy

Our apps are designed for professional adult users. We do not knowingly direct any product toward children under 13, and since we collect no data at all, we cannot inadvertently collect data from minors.

06
Policy Updates

If this policy changes, updated versions will be published here and reflected in each app's App Store listing. Material changes affecting your use will be communicated through app update notes. Last updated: March 2025.

Privacy Inquiries

Questions about privacy, data handling, or enterprise deployment? Contact us at privacy@newtonapps.org. We respond to all inquiries within 48 hours.
